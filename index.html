How to Play

Answer math questions as fast as you can — the quicker you answer, the more points you earn!

In multiplayer, answer 3 questions faster than your opponent in a row to unlock a power-up!

⚡ Power-Ups

❄️
Freeze
Freezes your opponent for 3 seconds — they can't answer!
💥
Blast
Blasts 10 points off your opponent's score!
💰
Steal
Steals 5 points from your opponent and adds them to yours!
